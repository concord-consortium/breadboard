SPARKS HELP: Calculating Resistor Tolerance

The precision of a resistor is called the tolerance. The tolerance of a resistor in a four-band resistor is indicated by the color of the fourth band. The tolerance of a resistor in a five-band resistor is indicated by the color of the fifth band. The tolerance is given as a percentage. To calculate the tolerance, you must first calculate the resistance using the color code.
