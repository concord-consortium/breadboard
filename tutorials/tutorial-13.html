Calculating Voltage in Series-Parallel Circuits

Figure 1. Series-parallel resistors.

In circuits that have resistors connected in both series and parallel, you will consider the circuit as two or more parts: the parallel parts and the series parts.

Use the series and parallel resistance formulas to determine the total resistance of these parts. Then, use the principles in Ohm's Law to calculate the voltage drops across each part.
